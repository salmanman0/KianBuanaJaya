About Us

Kian Buana Jaya

PT. Kian Buana Jaya is a company rooted in Indonesia, a fertile land rich in natural resources. Founded by Riki Piliano, we share a vision of creating sustainable agricultural solutions. Utilizing the potential of coconut coir, we produce high-quality cocopeat and coco fiber that have been recognized by farmers worldwide.

We believe that sustainable agriculture is the key to a better future. Therefore, we are committed to producing environmentally friendly products and supporting the welfare of farmers. Through partnerships with local farmers, we not only provide quality products but also contribute to the economic development of the community.

VISI

To become a global leader in the export of high-quality, sustainable cocopeat and coco fiber, meeting the demands of the European and Northern Asian markets while contributing to Indonesia's economic growth.

MISI

  • Deliver High-Quality Products To produce and export premium cocopeat and coco fiber that meet international standards, focusing on sustainability and innovation.
  • Commit to Sustainability To implement eco-friendly and sustainable production practices, preserving ecosystems, and supporting local farmers in resource management.
  • Enhance Global Competitiveness To build a strong distribution network in the European and Northern Asian markets, continuously expanding market reach through strategic partnerships and improved service quality.
